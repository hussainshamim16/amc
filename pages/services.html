Comprehensive Construction Solutions

At AMC Construction LLC, we are proud to be a trusted, veteran-owned contractor and Custom Home Builder in Columbia TN offering a full range of exterior and structural construction services. We serve homeowners, property managers, and commercial clients throughout Columbia and surrounding areas, including Franklin, Brentwood, College Grove, Leiper’s Fork, Thompson’s Station, and Santa Fe TN

With a strong commitment to integrity and quality workmanship, we deliver professional solutions for both new construction and property improvement projects. Whether you’re building a luxury custom home, managing a commercial facility, or renovating an investment property, our experienced team provides the skill, reliability, and attention to detail needed to complete your project to the highest standards. From roofing and concrete work to gutters, fencing, siding, and pressure washing, AMC Construction LLC delivers lasting results that enhance your property’s function, value, and curb appeal.
